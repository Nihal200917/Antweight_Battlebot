# Chassis
Sketch of chassis has been done and chassis prototype has been made on Fusaion 360 to test if all electronics fit before finalizing design, more parts are yet to be ordered like: New bBattery pack with charger, Servos for flipper.
Oblivion is meant to be a flipper robot. However due to deadline of June 27 and design constraint because of servos yet to be ordered. Oblivion might have to switch to a wedge.

## Sketch 
Sketch is not final because plausible design faults

### Problems
- Motor Mount may have problems because of space needed for soldering and for the back of motor to fit properly without damaging the motor
- Reciever Mount may have trouble because of the space needed for antennas to fit properly without getting ruptured
- Servo mount has not been made yet
- Wires for electronics may be conjested a little

### Advantanges
- Battery Mount seems good, offset of 0.3 mm should provide a snug fit and the notch should enable for the wires to connect with the rest of the electronics easily
- Offsets on all the mounts should provide a good fit after 3d printing is done. Offsets have not been mentioned in sketch. They are: Battery mount - 0.3mm offset; Reciever Mount - 0.3mm offset; Motor Mount - 0.2mm offset
